Hobbies and Interests

1. Cooking

I'm an avid home cook, having spent probably 20 years in the kitchen with my dad since I was just a little kid. People ask how I got so good at cooking... I think I have a knack for it, but really, it's just all the years of practice.

2. Golf

Another passion of mine is golf. I've played on and off since I was a kid and really got back into it with my friends now in my 20s. My favorite place to play (only partially because I get to play there for free) is the Granada Golf Course.

3. Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

About three years ago, I pushed myself *way* out of my comfort zone and decided to start Brazilian jiu-jitsu as a way to get into better shape and to learn some valuable lessons. I didn't really know what to expect or how long I would stick with it, but I was pleasantly surprised that I walked away with only a (really) broken finger.
